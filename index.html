Founder & Managing Partner

Shah Zaman S. Shami

Shah Zaman S. Shami is a distinguished legal professional with elite academic credentials and extensive corporate legal experience. A Lahore Board Topper and recipient of Academic Distinction from Government College University (GCU), Lahore, he earned his LLB from Punjab University Law College and later completed a Post Graduate Diploma in Environmental Law. He further advanced his international legal expertise through an LLM in International Business Law from the University of Bedfordshire, with specialized research in International Digital Trade Law.

As founder and leader of International Business Law Consultants LLP, Shah Zaman advises multinational corporations, local enterprises, and high-profile entities on complex domestic and cross-border legal matters. His corporate practice covers International Corporate Governance, International Commercial Litigation, International Commercial Arbitration, International Intellectual Property Law, and International Trade and Digital Trade Law.

Alongside his corporate practice, Shah Zaman has a strong record in public interest litigation and institutional service, including significant contributions to environmental jurisprudence and litigation in the Punjab University tree-cutting case. He also served as Secretary General of the International Law Foundation, leading legal research and reform initiatives. He currently serves as Legal Advisor to Kohala SMC Pvt. Ltd, Horeca Systems, and Kohala Systems, handling international organizational matters and ensuring compliance with Labour Department, EOBI, PESSI, LDA, and other regulatory authorities. He has represented leading companies including Jazz and ICON Builders, and also serves as Visiting Faculty at the International School of Law and Business.
